This project analyzes 3,500 e-commerce transactions from 2022-01-01 to 2024-12-31. The goal was to understand sales performance, profitability, and margin efficiency across products, categories, and regions.

Problem Statement

Although sales are strong, profitability is uneven across products and categories. Some items convert revenue into profit less efficiently, which means higher sales do not always translate into stronger margins.

Root Cause Analysis

  • Tablet has the lowest product margin at 16.36%.
  • Office has the weakest category margin at 16.94%.
  • 2024 margin dropped to 16.69%, below 2022 and 2023.
  • The issue is margin compression rather than weak demand.

Recommendations

  1. Review pricing and cost structure for low-margin products.
  2. Prioritize higher-margin products in promotions and bundles.
  3. Track profit margin monthly by product and region.
  4. Compare yearly performance to catch margin decline early.

Conclusion

The business is generating strong sales, but profits are not equally strong in every segment. Improving margin discipline will convert healthy revenue into better profitability.
